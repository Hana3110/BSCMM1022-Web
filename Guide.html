Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


1. What is BookLamp?

BookLamp is a free digital book platform that offers students and readers access to a collection of categorized books, summaries, and reviews.


2. Do I need to sign up to read books?

No, you can read most books without signing up. However, creating an account lets you leave reviews and save your favorites.


3. Are the books really free?

Yes! All books provided on BookLamp are free to read. Most are public domain or open-license books.


4. Can I download books?

Some books are available in PDF or ePub format for download. If available, the option will be shown on the book page.


5. How do I leave a review?

Visit the book’s page and scroll down to the review section. You must be signed in to leave a review.


6. How can I contact you?

You can use the Contact Us page or email us at support@booklamp.com.
